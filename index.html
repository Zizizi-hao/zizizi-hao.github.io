Zihao Zhang (张子豪)

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  GitHub

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at Southeast University and Shenzhen Loop Area Institution (SLAI), supervised by Prof. Hongyang Li and Prof. Ziyuan Pu.

Prior to joining Southeast University, I earned my M.S. (2024) in Transportation from Hunan University, supervised by Prof. Chenhui Liu.

My research focuses on autonomous driving, particularly 3D object detection, trajectory prediction, and driving behavior analysis.
